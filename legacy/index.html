Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need separate apps for teachers and students?

No. ClassBase is a single unified app. You get an admin panel login, and students get restricted student logins. Features unlock based on permissions.

Can I upload my own branding and logo?

Yes! ClassBase is fully white-label. We replace the app name, logo, and color scheme with your coaching institute's branding before deploying it to the Play Store.
